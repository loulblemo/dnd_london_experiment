## III. ACT III: THE FOUNDERS' OSSUARY
A dark, ruined crypt occupied by a splinter cult. The ossuary was sealed and hidden — its existence erased from the monastery's official records. Somehow, the acolytes of the Cult of the True Script found it anyway; how they learned of it is unclear, but they have been operating inside for long enough to have desecrated it thoroughly.

### The Warden's Hut
A low **wooden** hut — dark, weathered planks warped by decades of mountain cold — is built flush against the cliff face. Its door hangs open, the latch smashed. There is no crypt entrance visible anywhere on the cliff. The entrance is in here.

The acolytes used this as a bunkhouse. The room is wrecked: four or five makeshift sleeping pallets (straw sacks, bundled rags, stolen cloaks) are strewn across the floor, most of them kicked apart. Bowls, candle stubs, dirty bandages, a broken belt — the detritus of people living rough for weeks. The hearth is cold and choked with ash.

Standing against the entire east wall — untouched, immovable, utterly out of place among the squalor — is a **massive oak wardrobe**, dark with age, iron-banded, built flush against the cliff.

```
┌─────────────────────────────┐
│  [door, open]               │  ← North wall
│                             │
│  [pallets / bedding mess]   │
│  [pallets / bedding mess]   │  ← Centre & West
│  [hearth — cold]            │  ← South wall
│                             │
│ ██████ THE WARDROBE ██████  │  ← East wall (cliff-side)
└─────────────────────────────┘
```

#### The Wardrobe

The wardrobe is **locked** with a heavy iron bar-lock — the kind built to last, not to be decorative. It does not belong to the acolytes. It was here long before them, and they couldn't open it either.

*   **Force it (DC 20 Athletics):** The wood holds. The iron bands hold. If the players succeed, the frame splinters with a crack loud enough to hear at the bottom of a staircase — any occupants below are alerted.

--> Three extra accolites come armed with knives

*   **Pick the lock (DC 16 Thieves' Tools):** The mechanism is old but sound. Success opens it silently.
*   **Use the key:** The key hangs on a cord around the neck of the **dead warden-priest at the Pilgrim's Shrine** *(see Act II — The Shrine)*. It opens the lock with a single turn, no sound.

#### Inside the Wardrobe

The interior is empty except for a single iron peg and a moth-eaten habit still hanging from it. The back panel is raw cliff stone — but with a seam cut clean around it. It is a door.

Pressing firmly on the panel (no check once the wardrobe is open) causes it to swing inward on a stone pivot, releasing a breath of cold, dust-heavy air. **Stone steps descend steeply into darkness.** The air below smells of old wax and something faintly sweet — the rot of old bones. A dim light flickers far below.

### The Dungeon Crawl
*   **The Bell Trap:** DC 14 Perception to avoid alerting the cult.
*   **The Twitching Ward:** **3 Twitchers (Zombies)**. Use the *Lurching Miss* mechanic.
*   **Sarcophagus Maze:** **2 Crawling Torsos**. Focus on the *Ankle Grab* grapple.

#### The Charnel Alcove
A collapsed side-passage, reeking of grave rot. The floor is thick with old bones and split open burial urns. The door hangs off one hinge — something already forced its way in, or out.
*   **Enemies:** **3 Twitchers (Zombies)** — two are entangled in a collapsed shelf of urns and will only react on their second turn (effectively Surprised on round 1).
*   **Loot (hidden under a broken altar stone, DC 12 Investigation):**
    *   **2× Vial of Bitters** (Healing Potion, 2d4+2)
    *   **Whetstone of the Faithful** — a sharpening stone etched with Mitra's sunburst; grants **+1 to damage rolls** with one bladed weapon until the next long rest (one use, applied at camp).
    *   **Null-Silver Vial** — a stoppered vial of cold, mercury-like liquid that smells faintly of ozone; drinking it grants **+2 CA** for 1 hour. *(Simulation hint: it feels lighter than it should, and the liquid never warms to body temperature.)*

THE BELL WOULD ATTRACT 3 more accolites with the knives. 

### The Final Ritual: The Blood-Lock
> *"The Saint's light is choked by the Traitor's hand... the seal will break only when bathed in Betrayer's Blood."*

*   **Leader:** **Father Malachi**, leader of the **Cult of the True Script**.
*   **Minions:** **4 Weak Initiates**.
*   **The Puzzle:** The lead box only opens when bathed in **Betrayer's Blood** (blood from Malachi or his acolytes).
*   **The Clock:** 1-2 Zombies rise every round until the seal is broken.
